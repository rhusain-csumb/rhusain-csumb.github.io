Agile Methodology

Iterative development with frequent feedback and improvement.

SDLC Waterfall Agile DevOps

Overview

Agile focuses on delivering working software in small increments (often called iterations or sprints). Teams adjust plans based on feedback and changing requirements.

Key Ideas

  • Work is split into small, manageable tasks
  • Frequent releases and continuous improvement
  • Close collaboration with stakeholders
  • Adaptability when requirements change

Pros and Cons

Pros

  • Handles change well
  • Faster feedback reduces risk
  • Frequent delivery of usable features

Cons

  • Needs strong communication and teamwork
  • Scope can grow if not controlled
  • Harder to predict timeline for large projects
